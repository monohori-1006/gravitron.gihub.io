Mathematics Resources

Comprehensive notes and problem sets for university-level mathematics.

1. Matrices & Determinants

Key topics include matrix multiplication, finding the adjoint, and solving linear equations.

  • Cramer's Rule: Solving systems of equations using determinants: \[ x = \frac{\Delta_x}{\Delta}, \quad y = \frac{\Delta_y}{\Delta} \]
  • Matrix Inverse: \[ A^{-1} = \frac{1}{|A|} \text{adj}(A) \]

2. Calculus

Focusing on differential and integral calculus fundamentals.

  • Differentiation: Product rule, Quotient rule, and Chain rule.
  • Integration: \[ \int x^n \, dx = \frac{x^{n+1}}{n+1} + C \quad (n \neq -1) \]
